Welcome, Cryptographer! 🕵️‍♂️

This isn't just a calculator—it's a tutor. You're about to learn one of history's most famous manual ciphers.

1. The Matrix

Pick a Master Keyword. Watch the 5x5 grid transform. We use this matrix to find our secret coordinates. Because a 5x5 grid only holds 25 letters, I and J must share a cell to make the 26-letter alphabet fit!

2. Preparing for Battle

Playfair needs pairs. Type your message and hit Start Tutor. I'll show you how we clean the text and insert 'X' wedges to split up double letters.
(e.g., 'BALLOON' becomes 'BA LX LO ON')

3. The Geometry

Click Process Next Pair → to see the magic happen on the grid. Use ← Go Back to undo a step, or click any processed pair in the display to jump back to it.

  • Same Row? Slide right (or left to decrypt).
  • Same Column? Slide down (or up to decrypt).
  • The Rectangle? Swap corners!

4. Copy & Continue

After encrypting, use Copy & Open in Decrypt to send your ciphertext straight to the Decrypter tab — perfect for verifying your work!
